Top 10 Places to Visit in Baku

Travel Guide · Feb 8, 2026

Baku, the capital of Azerbaijan, is a fascinating destination where ancient history meets modern architecture. Located on the shores of the Caspian Sea, the city offers a unique mix of culture, tradition, and futuristic design.

1. Icherisheher (Old City)

The heart of Baku, Icherisheher is a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring narrow streets, historical mosques, and the iconic Maiden Tower. It is the best place to experience Azerbaijan’s rich past.

2. Flame Towers

These iconic skyscrapers dominate Baku’s skyline. At night, LED displays transform the towers into glowing flames, symbolizing the country’s ancient connection to fire.

3. Baku Boulevard & Highland Park

Enjoy a relaxing walk along the Caspian Sea or capture panoramic views of the city from Highland Park — one of the best photo spots in Baku.

A Complete Guide to Azerbaijani Cuisine

Food & Culture · Feb 5, 2026

Azerbaijani cuisine reflects the country’s geography and history. Rich in herbs, spices, and slow-cooked dishes, it offers flavors that are both comforting and unforgettable.

Plov – The National Dish

Plov is a fragrant rice dish cooked with saffron and served with lamb, dried fruits, or chestnuts. It is traditionally prepared for special occasions and celebrations.

Dolma & Qutab

Dolma consists of grape leaves or vegetables stuffed with minced meat and herbs. Qutab is a thin flatbread filled with greens, meat, or cheese and cooked on a griddle.

Traditional Sweets

Azerbaijani desserts such as shekerbura and pakhlava are deeply connected to local traditions, especially during the Novruz holiday.

The Best Photo Spots in Baku

Photography · Feb 1, 2026

Baku is a dream destination for photographers. The city offers contrasts between old and new, urban and natural landscapes, making it perfect for stunning photos.

Highland Park

This viewpoint provides a breathtaking panorama of the city skyline, Flame Towers, and the Caspian Sea — especially beautiful at sunset.

Icherisheher Streets

The stone walls, wooden balconies, and narrow alleys of the Old City create timeless photography opportunities.

Night Photography

Capture Baku after dark when the city lights reflect on modern buildings and the seaside boulevard comes alive.
